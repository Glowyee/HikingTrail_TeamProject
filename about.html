HIKE Scotland is an award winning website and service that helps adventurous people explore the great outdoors in Scotland. We offer detailed and bespoke hand-curated trail maps, hiking tours, trail reviews and photos crowdsourced from our community of hikers.
Founded in 2019 by two Psychologists, a Business Analyst and a Software developer with a passion for adventure and the great outdoors. Our headquarter can be found in Glasgow, Scotland and operates in an industry leading website that provides over a million hiking enthusiasts with the necessary tools and information they need to record their adventures and share with friends and loved ones. Our websites has been featured in the Guardian, Forbes, National Geographic, Great Walks Magazine and GQ Magazine, etc.
