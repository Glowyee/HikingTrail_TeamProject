General information

Located on the Isle of Skye, this is a very popular walk for both tourists and locals. As the name suggests, the walk follows pools and waterfalls, some more attractive to the eye than the other. Best to visit in the summer months unless you enjoy rain in which case you are welcome to visit them at any point in the year!

Terrain

This is an easy route to walk although at some points it might be muddy due to the surrounding water and the weather outside.

Getting there

Unfortunately the way to get there is by car. Alternatively, you could walk or cycle from a town with a bus stop. The starting point is the Fairy Pools car park.
